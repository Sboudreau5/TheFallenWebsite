REGAIN YOUR HONOR

THE FALLEN is a third person fantasy melee combat game. You play as a knight who awakens in The Land of the Fallen, a version of purgatory for dishonoured knights. Upon waking up, you come across a mysterious statue who guides you towards the three Fallen Guardians. If you’re able to defeat the Fallen Guardians, retrieve their helmets, and return them to The Statue, you will regain your honour and gain access to the eternal rest, the knight’s version of heaven.

SKILL BASED COMBAT

  • melee combat system including striking, blocking, and dodging maneuvers.

  • Three distinct Fallen Guardian characters, each with their own special ability and attack pattern AI.

  • A detailed and atmospheric environment.

EXPLORE THE WORLD OF THE FALLEN

The game takes place in the purgatory realm called “The Land of the Fallen”. The realm consists of four floating islands that each have a unique environment and are surrounded by a thick mysterious fog. The depths of the fog are seemingly endless.

The four island environments consist of:

  • Forest
  • Swamp
  • Cathedral
